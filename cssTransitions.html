The Transition Property

Click the text to see how the mouseDown() function is triggered. Clicking the text sets the color of the text to pink. The mouseUp() function is triggered when the mouse button is released, and sets the color to green.

Transition Property Hover

Hover your mouse over the tan colored box, and it will change, both in size, color, and shape!

Hover over the button, and it will only change color, not size, not shape.
